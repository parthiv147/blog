AI & ML in the Real World

  • What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?
    AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. It powers technologies like virtual assistants, recommendation systems, and self-driving cars.
  • How is Machine Learning (ML) different from AI?
    ML is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data and improve their performance without being explicitly programmed. It's used in spam filters, fraud detection, and personalized ads.
